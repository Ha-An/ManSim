# Output Key Glossary

- `summary`: short curation synthesis.
- `daily_updates`: reusable day-level facts or lessons.
- `managed_object_updates`: updates grouped by items, workers, equipment, and queues.
- `manager_lessons`: lessons grouped by strategist, reviewer, and curator perspectives.
- `links`: useful Obsidian-style target links.
- `graph_hints`: likely nodes or edges for the knowledge graph.
- `open_questions`: uncertainties to revisit later.
